Preface

As Mendeley decided to retire their reference management tool Mendeley Desktop and replace it with the immature Mendeley Reference Manager that is missing many important functions, more and more users complain about its user-unfriendly-ness (For example, see discussions on Reddit). I also have an answer below a Zhihu question sharing the archive of old Mendeley desktop installers that received a large amount of likes.

I am sharing the old installers for free as an internet archive, hosted in my Github repository. Please keep in mind that the copyright belongs to Mendeley, and I hold no responsibility for data lost or any damage caused by incorrect installation.
