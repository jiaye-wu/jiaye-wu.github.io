[Invited Talk] Enhancement of linear and nonlinear thermo-optic effects by epsilon-near-zero conditions

Created in December 26, 2023

2023   ·   epsilon-near-zero   nonlinear optics   nanophotonics   invited talk   ·   talks   research blog

On December 26th, 2023, I was Invited by The 2nd International Laser Forum for Young Scholars, Shanghai Institute of Optics and Fine Machines (SIOM), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) to present a talk on Enhancement of linear and nonlinear thermo-optic effects by epsilon-near-zero conditions at SIOM, CAS, Shanghai, China. (Wu* et al., 2024; Wu* et al., 2024)

This talk was based on my recent publication in Nature Communications.
