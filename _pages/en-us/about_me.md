## Jiaye Wu

**Assistant Professor, PI, Ph.D. Supervisor**

Jiaye is currently an Assistant Professor at the Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), <img src="/assets/img/university_logos/CityUHK-DG.png" style="height: 1.1em; "> [City University of Hong Kong (Dongguan)](https://www.cityu-dg.edu.cn/en/home.html), China. Before joining CityUHK-DG, he was a post-doctoral fellow (collaborateur scientifique) at the Photonics Systems Laboratory (PHOSL), <img src="/assets/img/university_logos/EPFL.png" style="height: 0.8em; "> [École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne](https://www.epfl.ch/) (EPFL, Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Lausanne). His expertise and current research interests span ultrafast light-matter-interactions, nonlinear optics and nanophotonics in epsilon-near-zero materials, fibre, fibre laser, and waveguide network design, functional thin film design, and non-Hermitian optics. Throughout his academic trajectory, he has consistently exemplified a commitment to outstanding scholarship. His prolific publications include 50+ academic papers in peer-reviewed esteemed journals such as _Nature Communications_, _Laser & Photonics Reviews_, etc. He served as a peer-reviewer for _Laser & Photonics Reviews_, _Advanced Photonics_, etc., and a co-reviewer for _Nature_, _Light: Science & Applications_, etc.
